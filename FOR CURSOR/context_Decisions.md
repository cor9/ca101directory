**📋 SUBMISSION PROCESS:**
1. **User visits homepage** → Sees pricing and call-to-action buttons
2. **Clicks "Submit Your Listing"** → Redirected to `/submit` (requires login)
3. **Fills out form** → Only "Listing Name" required (admin fills rest)
4. **Form submits to Airtable** → Creates record with "Pending" status
5. **Admin reviews in Airtable** → Manually fills all other fields
6. **Admin approves** → Changes status to "Live"
7. **Listing appears** → Shows on homepage (filtered by "Live" status)
