# Vendor Success Academy

# Hiring Process

## Goal

Hire relationship-focused representatives who can consistently introduce
entertainment professionals to the Child Actor 101 Directory.

## Hiring Steps

1.  Application
2.  Initial review
3.  20-minute video interview
4.  Mock vendor conversation
5.  Reference check (optional)
6.  Offer
7.  Contractor agreement & NDA
8.  Vendor Success Academy onboarding

## What We Look For

-   Excellent communication
-   Coachability
-   Reliability
-   Professionalism
-   Organization
-   Genuine interest in helping small businesses

Sales experience is preferred but not required.
