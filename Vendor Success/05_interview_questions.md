# Interview Guide

## Core Questions

1.  Tell me about yourself.
2.  Why are you interested in Child Actor 101?
3.  Describe a time you persuaded someone without being pushy.
4.  How do you stay organized when following up with multiple people?
5.  How would you respond if a vendor said, "I'm already getting enough
    business."
6.  Tell me about a time you handled rejection.
7.  How many hours each week can you consistently dedicate?

## Mock Exercise

Role-play a 5-minute introductory call with a headshot photographer who
has never heard of the directory.

Evaluate: - Listening - Confidence - Clarity - Professionalism - Ability
to build rapport
