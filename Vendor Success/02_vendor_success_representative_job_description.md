# Vendor Success Representative

## Official Job Description

### Position Type

-   Independent Contractor (1099)
-   Commission-Based
-   Remote
-   Flexible Schedule

## Position Summary

Vendor Success Representatives introduce entertainment industry
professionals to the Child Actor 101 Directory and help them select the
listing that best supports their business.

Representatives are ambassadors for the Child Actor 101 brand and are
expected to build genuine relationships while growing one of the
industry's most trusted vendor resources.

## Primary Responsibilities

-   Prospect and contact potential vendors.
-   Explain directory features and pricing.
-   Assist vendors through the enrollment process.
-   Follow up with interested prospects.
-   Maintain accurate communication records.
-   Represent Child Actor 101 professionally and ethically.

## Performance Expectations

-   Consistent outreach
-   Professional communication
-   Honest representation of products and services
-   Compliance with approved messaging
-   Protection of confidential information

No minimum sales quota is required. Continued participation is based
upon professionalism, activity, and overall performance.

## Compensation

### Base Commission

  Listing                          Commission
  ------------------------------ ------------
  Standard Monthly                       \$10
  Pro Monthly                            \$20
  Founding Standard (6 Months)           \$35
  Founding Pro (6 Months)                \$70
  Standard Annual                        \$80
  Pro Annual                            \$160

### Monthly Performance Bonus

Performance bonuses are awarded based on the highest qualifying sales
tier achieved during a calendar month.

Bonuses are **not cumulative**.

At least **25% of qualifying sales must be Founding (6-Month) or Annual
Vendor Listings.**

    Qualified Sales   Bonus
  ----------------- -------
             10--14   \$100
             15--19   \$200
             20--29   \$350
                30+   \$600

## Training

Representatives receive:

-   Lead lists
-   Sales scripts
-   Product training
-   Messaging templates
-   Ongoing support
