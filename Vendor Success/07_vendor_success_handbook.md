# Vendor Success Handbook

## Mission

Help trusted entertainment professionals connect with families who are
actively searching for quality industry resources.

We are not high-pressure salespeople. We are educators, consultants, and
relationship builders.

## Core Values

-   Lead with honesty.
-   Build trust before asking for a sale.
-   Never oversell features.
-   Protect the Child Actor 101 reputation.
-   Every interaction should leave the vendor feeling respected.

## Success Formula

Know the product → Listen → Recommend the right plan → Follow up
consistently.

## Daily Expectations

-   Review assigned leads.
-   Perform outreach.
-   Follow up on previous conversations.
-   Record activity.
-   Celebrate wins and learn from losses.

## Communication Standards

-   Professional
-   Friendly
-   Prompt
-   Helpful
-   Never argumentative

## Vendor Experience

Every vendor should feel that joining the directory is the beginning of
a partnership---not the end of a sale.

## The Golden Rule

If you would not recommend the listing to a friend or family member, do
not recommend it to a vendor.
