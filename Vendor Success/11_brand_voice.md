# Vendor Success Academy

# Module 11: The Child Actor 101 Brand Voice

## Our Promise

We educate first and sell second.

## Voice

-   Honest
-   Encouraging
-   Professional
-   Practical
-   Never pushy

## Do

-   Ask questions.
-   Listen before recommending.
-   Explain benefits clearly.
-   Be transparent.

## Don't

-   Pressure vendors.
-   Use fear-based sales.
-   Exaggerate traffic or results.
-   Speak negatively about competitors.
