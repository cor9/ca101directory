# Vendor Success Academy

# Objection Handling

## "I already get enough business."

That's great to hear. Many of our vendors join because they want to
strengthen their online presence, build credibility, and stay visible to
new families---not because business is slow.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## "I don't have the budget."

I understand. We offer monthly, Founding, and annual options so
businesses can choose what fits their budget and goals.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## "I need to think about it."

Absolutely. Is there anything specific you'd like more information about
before deciding?

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## "What makes your directory different?"

Child Actor 101 serves a highly targeted audience of parents and young
actors who are actively looking for trusted entertainment resources. Our
focus is quality, education, and community.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## "Can you guarantee clients?"

No. We never guarantee bookings or business. The directory is designed
to increase visibility and connect vendors with families searching for
industry services.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Remember

Objections are usually requests for more information---not rejection.

Stay curious, answer honestly, and never pressure someone into joining.
