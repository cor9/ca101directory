# Product Knowledge

## What is Child Actor 101?

Child Actor 101 is an educational ecosystem serving families pursuing
television and film careers through training, coaching, articles,
podcasts, tools, and a trusted vendor directory.

## Directory Purpose

The directory connects parents and actors with reputable entertainment
professionals.
