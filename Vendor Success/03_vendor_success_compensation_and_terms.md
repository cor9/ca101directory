# Vendor Success Representative

## Compensation & Contractor Terms

## Contractor Status

Vendor Success Representatives are independent contractors and not
employees of Child Actor 101.

Contractors are responsible for their own taxes, insurance, equipment,
internet service, and business expenses.

## Commission Eligibility

Commissions are earned only after:

-   Customer payment has successfully processed.
-   Payment has cleared.
-   The transaction has not been refunded, disputed, cancelled, or
    charged back.

No commissions are paid on refunded, fraudulent, disputed, cancelled, or
chargeback transactions.

## Performance Bonus

One performance bonus may be earned each calendar month.

Bonuses are not cumulative and are based on the highest qualifying tier
achieved.

To qualify, at least 25% of closed sales must be Founding (6-Month) or
Annual Vendor Listings.

## Lead Ownership

All leads, vendors, customer information, and business relationships
remain the exclusive property of Child Actor 101.

## Marketing Standards

Representatives agree to:

-   Use approved messaging.
-   Make truthful representations.
-   Protect confidential information.
-   Conduct themselves professionally.

## Payment Schedule

Qualified commissions and bonuses are paid monthly following
reconciliation of completed transactions.

## Changes to Compensation

Child Actor 101 reserves the right to modify future commission rates,
incentives, bonuses, and compensation plans with written notice.
Previously earned commissions will not be affected.

## Termination

Either party may terminate the contractor relationship at any time.

Earned commissions on qualified sales completed before termination will
be paid according to this compensation plan.
