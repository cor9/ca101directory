# Vendor Success Academy

# Module 12: Consultative Selling

## Philosophy

Our role is to determine whether the directory is a good fit.

## Framework

1.  Learn about the business.
2.  Understand goals.
3.  Recommend the appropriate plan.
4.  Handle questions honestly.
5.  Follow up respectfully.

Success is measured by long-term vendor satisfaction, not simply closing
a sale.
