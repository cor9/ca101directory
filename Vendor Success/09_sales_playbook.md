# Vendor Success Playbook

## Philosophy

We don't pressure vendors into joining.

We help them determine whether the directory is a good fit for their
business.

## Outreach Process

1.  Research the vendor.
2.  Personalize the first message.
3.  Explain why you reached out.
4.  Introduce Child Actor 101.
5.  Ask permission to continue.
6.  Recommend the appropriate listing.
7.  Follow up if needed.

## Conversation Framework

### Discover

Ask about their business and goals.

### Educate

Explain how the directory works.

### Recommend

Match the appropriate plan.

### Close

Invite them to join.

### Follow Up

Most sales happen after the initial conversation.

## Best Practices

-   Personalize outreach.
-   Listen more than you talk.
-   Focus on benefits instead of features.
-   Keep messages concise.
-   Always follow up.

## Never Do

-   Pressure vendors.
-   Make promises you cannot verify.
-   Criticize competitors.
-   Misrepresent traffic or results.
