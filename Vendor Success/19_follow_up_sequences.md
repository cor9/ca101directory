# Vendor Success Academy

# Module 14: Follow-Up Sequence

## Day 1

Initial email or direct message.

## Day 4

Friendly follow-up referencing the original message.

## Day 8

Provide additional value: - Explain directory benefits. - Share a
feature they may find useful. - Answer common questions.

## Day 14

Final follow-up.

Keep it short, professional, and leave the door open for future
conversations.

## Follow-Up Principles

-   Never pressure.
-   Always add value.
-   Respect a "no."
-   Stop outreach if requested.
-   Record every interaction in the CRM.
